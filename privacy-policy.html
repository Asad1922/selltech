Privacy Policy

Your privacy is important to us. This policy explains what information we collect and how we use it.

1. Introduction

Welcome to SellTech ("we," "our," or "us"). We are a software development and digital marketing agency committed to protecting the privacy and security of our clients, website visitors, and service users. This Privacy Policy outlines our practices concerning the collection, use, and disclosure of your information through our website (selltech.pro), services, and any other interactions you may have with us. By accessing our website or using our services, you agree to the terms of this Privacy Policy. We encourage you to read this policy carefully to understand our information practices. We may update this policy from time to time, and we will notify you of any significant changes by posting the new policy on our website and updating the effective date. Your continued use of our services after such changes constitutes your acceptance of the new policy.

2. Information We Collect

We collect information to provide and improve our services, communicate with you, and for other purposes described in this policy. The types of information we collect depend on your interactions with us.

2.1. Personal Information You Provide

This is information that you voluntarily provide to us, which can be used to identify you personally. Examples include:

  • Contact Information: When you fill out a contact form, request a quote, or subscribe to our newsletter, we may collect your name, email address, phone number, and company name.
  • Project Details: When you inquire about our services, you may provide us with details about your project, business needs, and objectives. This can include business plans, technical requirements, and other proprietary information.
  • Account Information: If you become a client, we will collect information necessary to create and manage your account, including billing information such as credit card details or other payment information, which is processed by our secure third-party payment processors.
  • Communication Data: We collect information when you communicate with us, including records of your correspondence via email, phone, or our website's chat features.

2.2. Information We Collect Automatically

When you visit our website or use our services, we automatically collect certain information about your device and usage. This information helps us understand how our services are being used and how we can improve them.

  • Log Data: Our servers automatically record information ("Log Data") created by your use of the Services. Log Data may include information such as your IP address, browser type, operating system, the referring web page, pages visited, location, your mobile carrier, device information, and search terms.
  • Cookies and Similar Technologies: We use cookies, web beacons, and similar tracking technologies to collect information about your browsing activities over time and across different websites. Cookies are small data files stored on your hard drive or in device memory that help us improve our Services and your experience, see which areas and features of our Services are popular, and count visits. You can usually set your browser to remove or reject browser cookies. Please note that if you choose to remove or reject cookies, this could affect the availability and functionality of our services.

2.3. Information from Third Parties

We may receive information about you from third-party sources, such as social media platforms if you interact with us through those platforms, and from third-party data providers. We may combine this information with other information we have collected about you. This helps us to update, expand, and analyze our records, identify new customers, and provide products and services that may be of interest to you.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect for various purposes, including:

  • To Provide and Manage Our Services: We use your information to deliver our software development, digital marketing, and other services as requested by you. This includes managing your projects, providing customer support, and processing payments.
  • To Communicate with You: We use your contact information to send you project updates, respond to your inquiries, send you marketing communications (where permitted by law), and provide you with other information that may be of interest to you.
  • To Improve Our Services: We analyze information about how you use our services to understand user behavior, improve our website and service offerings, and develop new features and functionality.
  • For Marketing and Advertising: We may use your information to personalize the advertising and content you see, to measure the effectiveness of our marketing campaigns, and to send you promotional materials about our services. You can opt-out of marketing communications at any time.
  • For Security and Compliance: We use information to protect the security and integrity of our services, to prevent fraud and other illegal activities, and to comply with our legal obligations, including responding to lawful requests from public authorities.

4. How We Share Your Information

We do not sell your personal information. We may share your information with third parties in the following circumstances:

  • With Service Providers: We may share your information with third-party vendors, consultants, and other service providers who need access to such information to carry out work on our behalf. These may include hosting providers, payment processors, analytics companies, and marketing partners. These service providers are authorized to use your personal information only as necessary to provide these services to us.
  • For Legal Reasons: We may disclose your information if we believe it is necessary to comply with a law, regulation, legal process, or governmental request; to protect the safety of any person; to address fraud, security, or technical issues; or to protect our rights or property.
  • Business Transfers: In the event that SellTech is involved in a merger, acquisition, financing due diligence, reorganization, bankruptcy, receivership, sale of company assets, or transition of service to another provider, your information may be sold or transferred as part of such a transaction as permitted by law and/or contract.
  • With Your Consent: We may share your information with third parties when we have your explicit consent to do so.

5. Data Security

We take the security of your data very seriously. We implement a variety of security measures to maintain the safety of your personal information when you place an order or enter, submit, or access your personal information. These measures include the use of encryption, firewalls, and secure socket layer (SSL) technology. However, no method of transmission over the Internet, or method of electronic storage, is 100% secure. Therefore, we cannot guarantee its absolute security. We encourage you to take steps to protect yourself against unauthorized access to your password, phone, and computer by, among other things, signing off after using a shared computer, choosing a robust password that nobody else knows or can easily guess, and keeping your log-in and password private.

6. Your Data Protection Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the following rights regarding your personal information:

  • The right to access: You have the right to request copies of your personal data.
  • The right to rectification: You have the right to request that we correct any information you believe is inaccurate. You also have the right to request that we complete information you believe is incomplete.
  • The right to erasure: You have the right to request that we erase your personal data, under certain conditions.
  • The right to restrict processing: You have the right to request that we restrict the processing of your personal data, under certain conditions.
  • The right to object to processing: You have the right to object to our processing of your personal data, under certain conditions.
  • The right to data portability: You have the right to request that we transfer the data that we have collected to another organization, or directly to you, under certain conditions.

If you would like to exercise any of these rights, please contact us at our provided contact information. We will respond to your request within a reasonable timeframe.

7. Children's Privacy

Our Services are not directed to individuals under the age of 16. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 16. If we become aware that a child under 16 has provided us with personal information, we will take steps to delete such information. If you become aware that a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us immediately.

8. International Data Transfers

Your information, including personal data, may be transferred to — and maintained on — computers located outside of your state, province, country, or other governmental jurisdiction where the data protection laws may differ from those from your jurisdiction. If you are located outside the United States and choose to provide information to us, please note that we transfer the data, including personal data, to the United States and process it there. Your consent to this Privacy Policy followed by your submission of such information represents your agreement to that transfer.
